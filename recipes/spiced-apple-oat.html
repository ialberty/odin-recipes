Spiced Apple Oat Pots

Description

These comforting pots of apple, oats and cinnamon are comforting and warming.

This is one that all the family will love.

Ingredients

  • 100g rolled oats
  • 250ml almond milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1 red apple, grated
  • 1 tablespoon of jumbo raisins

Steps

  1. Add the oats, almond milk, cinnamon, and ginger to a pan and cook over a medium heat for 3 minutes.
  2. When the oats appear creamy, stir through the apple and raisins, then cook for a further 2-3 minutes.
  3. Serve immediately.
