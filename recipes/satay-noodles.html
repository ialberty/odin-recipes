Satay Noodles

Description

If you're a lover of peanut butter, this bowl of creamy noodles is designed for you.

With the perfect balance of heat, salt, and bitters, you'll have this on your table before you can run out to grab a hot box!

Ingredients

  • For the sauce:
    • 4 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
    • 2 teaspoons dark soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon dried chilli flakes
    • juice of 1 unwaxed lime
  • For the noodles:
    • 1 tablespoon of sunflower oil
    • handful of sugar snap peas, halved
    • 1 small carrot, finely sliced
    • 2 spring onions, chopped
    • 2 packs of soft ready-to-wok noodles
    • 2 teaspoons of white sesame seeds
    • small handful of fresh coriander
    • 1 red chilli, deseeded and finely sliced

Steps

  1. To make the sauce, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, and chilli flakes along with 200ml cold water in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in the lime juice to form a smooth paste.
  3. For the noodles, heat the oil in a wok over a high heat.
  4. When the oil is hot, throw in the vegetables and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Separate the noodles and add them to the wok.
  6. Pour in the peanut sauce and stir-fry for a further minute.
  7. Sprinkle over the sesame seeds, coriander, and chilli.
  8. Serve immediately.
